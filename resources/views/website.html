About Us

Welcome to Quaid-e-Azam Public High School

Quaid-e-Azam Public High School in Sambrial offers a unique educational experience with a focus on affordability and quality. Our school is led by Director Zamin Ali, an MSc in Anthropology, who is dedicated to providing a nurturing environment for students to thrive.

We pride ourselves on our affordable fee structure, ensuring that quality education is accessible to all. Located in Wahab Town near Nawab Pura, Sambrial, our school offers separate classes for boys and girls, providing a comfortable learning environment for all students.
